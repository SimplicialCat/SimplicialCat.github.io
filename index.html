Maths

My supervisor is Prof. Lin Chen.

My mathematical interests lie in

  • initially, differential geometry, algebraic topology;
  • later, category theory, topos theory, categorical logic,
  • homotopy theory, homotopy type theory;
  • and recently, the geometric Langlands program.
